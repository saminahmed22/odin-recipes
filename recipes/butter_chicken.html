Butter chicken Recipe

Butter Chicken: A Creamy Delight of Indian Cuisine

Butter chicken, or Murgh Makhani, is a rich and creamy dish originating from Indian cuisine, loved worldwide for its velvety texture and aromatic flavors. Tender chicken pieces are simmered in a luscious tomato-based gravy infused with butter, cream, and traditional spices like garam masala. This indulgent dish pairs perfectly with naan or rice, making it a comforting and satisfying meal. A symbol of culinary elegance, butter chicken strikes the perfect balance between mild spice and creamy decadence.

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken (boneless, cut into pieces)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 teaspoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons yogurt
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps:

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat butter in a pan and sauté the marinated chicken until lightly browned. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add more butter and sauté onions until golden brown.
  4. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook until fragrant.
  5. Add tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the mixture.
  6. Mix in garam masala, salt, and a splash of water if needed.
  7. Return the chicken to the pan, stir well, and let it simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  8. Pour in heavy cream and stir to create a rich, creamy texture.
  9. Cook for another 2-3 minutes and garnish with fresh cilantro.
  10. Serve hot with naan or rice.
