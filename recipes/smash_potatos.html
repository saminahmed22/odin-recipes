Smash potato Recipe

Smashed Potatoes: Crispy, Flavorful, and Easy to Make

Smashed potatoes are a delightful and simple dish that transforms boiled potatoes into crispy, golden bites of comfort. These potatoes are lightly smashed to create a rustic texture, then seasoned with herbs, garlic, and olive oil before being roasted to perfection. With a soft interior and crispy edges, smashed potatoes are a versatile side dish that pairs wonderfully with any meal, offering a satisfying combination of flavors and textures.

Ingredients:

  • 500g baby potatoes
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary or thyme
  • Salt to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Optional: Grated parmesan cheese for garnish

Steps:

  1. Boil the baby potatoes in salted water until tender, about 15-20 minutes. Drain and let them cool slightly.
  2. Preheat your oven to 200°C (400°F) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. Place the boiled potatoes on the baking sheet and gently smash them with the back of a spoon or a potato masher.
  4. Drizzle olive oil over the smashed potatoes and sprinkle with garlic, rosemary, salt, and black pepper.
  5. Bake in the oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the edges are golden and crispy.
  6. Remove from the oven and optionally sprinkle with grated parmesan cheese.
  7. Serve hot as a side dish or snack.
